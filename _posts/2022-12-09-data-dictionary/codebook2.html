PREGENDYR

For women who had a pregnancy end in miscarriage, abortion, or stillbirth, PREGENDYR reports the calendar year the last such pregnancy ended.

Enumerators were instructed to enter the date as January 1st, 2020 if the respondent did not give a response to the associated survey question. IPUMS has recoded these cases to “No response or missing.”

The question associated with this variable was included in the female questionnaire.

Comparability

This variable reports whether the woman has had a pregnancy end in miscarriage, abortion, or stillbirth over the course of her life. Longitudinal baseline samples, and the cross sectional samples derived from them, have a reference period of the extent of the contraceptive calendar, usually of 3 years. Phase 2 samples, and the cross sectional samples derived from them, do not have a reference period limit.
