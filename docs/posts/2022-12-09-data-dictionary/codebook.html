ABORDUR

For women who have successfully ended a pregnancy, ABORDUR, together with ABORDURVAL, reports how long she had been pregnant when she ended the pregnancy.

Note that this variable reports only a specific unit of time i.e., weeks or months, not the actual duration of the interval. The specific number of weeks or months is reported in ABORDURVAL. Users must use both ABORDUR and ABORDURVAL to determine the exact amount of time the woman had been pregnant.

The question associated with this variable was included in the female questionnaire.

Gestational age when removed pregnancy (units)
Weeks 11 (0.2%)
Months 39 (0.6%)
Don’t know 2 (<0.1%)
NIU (not in universe) 6,568 (99%)
Unknown 89,861
